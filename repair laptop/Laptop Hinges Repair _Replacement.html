    Types of Hinges Problem

    Looseness or Wobbling

    If your laptop screen feels loose or wobbly when you open it, it’s likely due to general wear and tear, frequent use, or a design flaw in the build quality.

    Stiffness or Tightness

    On the other hand, if you’re struggling to open or close the laptop because of stiffness, this could be caused by a buildup of dirt, misaligned parts, or simply a lack of lubrication.

    Hinge Breakage

    Physical damage, such as a broken hinge, often happens after accidental drops, rough handling, or using too much force on the lid.

    Lid Misalignment

    If your laptop lid doesn’t align properly when closed, it’s likely due to uneven pressure on the hinges, an impact, or internal misalignment.

    Cracking Sounds

    Lastly, if you hear cracking or popping noises when opening or closing the laptop, it could be a sign of friction between hinge components, worn-out parts, or a lack of proper lubrication.
